My Second Blog Post

AI can be divided into two categories: narrow or weak AI and general or strong AI. Narrow AI is designed to perform a specific task, such as recognizing faces or playing chess, while general AI is capable of performing any intellectual task that a human can do. While general AI is still largely theoretical, narrow AI is already being used in a variety of applications. Machine learning is a subset of AI that involves training algorithms to make predictions based on data. Deep learning is a type of machine learning that uses neural networks to model complex patterns in data. Natural language processing (NLP) is another subset of AI that involves teaching machines to understand and interpret human language.
